About the Show

Built for Washington

Amplify Washington is a daily weekday community broadcast produced by HedgePost LLC — the same team behind Amplify MP in Mount Pleasant. We exist to give Washington, Iowa a dedicated voice: a place where local stories get told, local events get promoted, and local people get the spotlight they deserve.

Every weekday we go live to highlight what's happening in Washington and the surrounding area — from Washington Community School District athletics and activities, to Chamber of Commerce news, to the neighbors and businesses that make this community run.

Washington deserves a show that puts Washington first. That's exactly what we're building.

We stream live on YouTube and Facebook, so you can watch from anywhere — your phone, your desk, or your TV. Can't catch it live? Every episode is available on replay anytime.

Amplify Washington is a HedgePost LLC production. HedgePost also operates Amplify MP in Mount Pleasant and covers sports across Southeast Iowa through MP Sports Livestream.
