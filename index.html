About the Show

Built for Washington

Amplify Washington is a daily weekday community broadcast — a dedicated voice for Washington, Iowa. We exist to make sure local stories get told, local events get promoted, and local people get the spotlight they deserve.

Every weekday we go live to highlight what's happening in Washington — from Washington Community School District athletics and activities, to Chamber of Commerce news, to the neighbors and businesses that make this community run.

Washington deserves a show that puts Washington first. That's exactly what we're building.

We stream live on YouTube and Facebook, so you can watch from anywhere — your phone, your desk, or your TV. Can't catch it live? Every episode stays in our Show Archive.
